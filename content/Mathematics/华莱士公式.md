# Wallis product

$$
\prod_ {n=1}^{\infty} \frac{2n}{2n-1} \frac{2n}{2n+1} = \frac{2}{1} \frac{2}{3} \frac{4}{3} \frac{4}{5} \frac{6}{5} \frac{6}{7} \dots = \frac{\pi}{2}
$$

# 点火公式（常用）

$$
\int _{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin ^{n} x \thinspace \mathrm{d}x =\int _{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\cos ^{n} x \thinspace \mathrm{d}x = \begin{cases}
\frac{n-1}{n} \frac{n-3}{n-2} \dots \frac{3}{4} \frac{1}{2} \frac{\pi}{2},n为偶数 \\
\frac{n-1}{n} \frac{n-3}{n-2} \dots \frac{4}{5} \frac{2}{3} 1,n为奇数 
\end{cases}
$$
