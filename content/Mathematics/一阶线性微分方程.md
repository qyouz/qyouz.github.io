# 一阶齐次线性微分方程

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x} + P(x)y = 0
$$

## 通解

$$
y = Ce^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x }
$$

## 推导过程

$$
\begin{gather}
\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x} + P(x)y = 0 \\ \\
\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x} = -P(x)y \\ \\
\frac{1}{y} \mathrm{d}y = -P(x) \mathrm{d}x \\ \\
\int \frac{1}{y} \thinspace \mathrm{d}y = - \int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x \\ \\
\ln |y| = - \int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x \\ \\
y = e^{- \int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x}
\end{gather}
$$

# 一阶非齐次线性微分方程

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x} + P(x)y=Q(x)
$$

# 通解

$$
y = e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } \left[ \int Q(x)e^{\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } \thinspace \mathrm{d}x +C ) \right]
$$

# 推导过程

使用常数易变法，将 [一阶齐次线性微分方程通解](#通解) 中的常数 $C$ 换成 $x$ 的未知函数 $u(x)$ ,于是

$$
\begin{gather}
y = u(x)e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } \\ \\
\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}  = u'(x) e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } - u(x)P(x)e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x }
\end{gather}
$$

带入原方程

$$
\begin{gather}
u'(x) e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } - u(x)P(x)e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } + P(x)u(x)e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } = Q(x) \\ \\
u'(x) e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } = Q(x) \\ \\
u'(x) = Q(x)e^{\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } \\ \\
u(x) = \int Q(x)e^{\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } \thinspace \mathrm{d}x 
\end{gather}
$$

将 $u(x)$ 带入 [一阶齐次线性微分方程通解](#通解) 得

$$
y = e^{-\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } \left[ \int Q(x)e^{\int P(x) \thinspace \mathrm{d}x } \thinspace \mathrm{d}x +C ) \right]
$$

# 伯努利方程

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x} + P(x)y = Q(x)y^{n} \quad (n\neq 0,1)
$$

令 $z = y^{1-n}$

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x} + (1-n)P(x)z = (1-n)Q(x)
$$
