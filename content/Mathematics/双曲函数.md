# 双曲函数定义 (Hyperbolic Functions)

- **双曲正弦 (Hyperbolic Sine):**

$$
\sinh x = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2}
$$

- **双曲余弦 (Hyperbolic Cosine):**

$$
\cosh x = \frac{e^x + e^{-x}}{2}
$$

 
- **双曲正切 (Hyperbolic Tangent):**

$$
\tanh x = \frac{\sinh x}{\cosh x} = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} = \frac{e^{2x} - 1}{e^{2x} + 1}
$$

- **双曲余切 (Hyperbolic Cotangent):** 当 $x \neq 0$

$$
\coth x = \frac{\cosh x}{\sinh x} = \frac{e^x + e^{-x}}{e^x - e^{-x}} = \frac{e^{2x} + 1}{e^{2x} - 1}
$$

  
- **双曲正割 (Hyperbolic Secant):**

$$
\text{sech } x = \frac{1}{\cosh x} = \frac{2}{e^x + e^{-x}} = \frac{2e^x}{e^{2x} + 1}
$$

 
- **双曲余割 (Hyperbolic Cosecant):** 当 $x \neq 0$

$$
\text{csch } x = \frac{1}{\sinh x} = \frac{2}{e^x - e^{-x}} = \frac{2e^x}{e^{2x} - 1}
$$

# 反双曲函数定义 (Inverse Hyperbolic Functions)

反双曲函数是双曲函数的**反函数**。它们的定义为：

- **反双曲正弦 (Inverse Hyperbolic Sine):**

$$
\text{arsinh}(x) = \ln(x + \sqrt{x^2 + 1})
$$

- **反双曲余弦 (Inverse Hyperbolic Cosine):**

$$
\text{arcosh}(x) = \ln(x + \sqrt{x^2 - 1}); \quad x \geq 1
$$

- **反双曲正切 (Inverse Hyperbolic Tangent):**

$$
\text{artanh}(x) = \frac{1}{2} \ln\left(\frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\right); \quad |x| < 1
$$

- **反双曲余切 (Inverse Hyperbolic Cotangent):**

$$
\text{arcoth}(x) = \frac{1}{2} \ln\left(\frac{x + 1}{x - 1}\right); \quad |x| > 1
$$

- **反双曲正割 (Inverse Hyperbolic Secant):**

$$
\text{arsech}(x) = \ln\left(\frac{1}{x} + \frac{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}{x}\right); \quad 0 < x \leq 1
$$

- **反双曲余割 (Inverse Hyperbolic Cosecant):**

$$
\text{arcsch}(x) = \ln\left(\frac{1}{x} + \frac{\sqrt{1 + x^2}}{|x|}\right); \quad x \neq 0
$$
