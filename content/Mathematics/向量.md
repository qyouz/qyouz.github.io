# 数量积 (Dot product)

$$
\boldsymbol{a} \cdot \boldsymbol{b} = |\boldsymbol{a}| |\boldsymbol{b}| \cos \alpha
$$

$$
\boldsymbol{a} \cdot \boldsymbol{b} = \sum _{i=1} ^{n} a_{i}b_{i}
$$

# 向量积(Cross product)

$$
\boldsymbol{a} \times \boldsymbol{b} = \begin{vmatrix}
\boldsymbol{i} & \boldsymbol{j} & \boldsymbol{k}  \\
a_{x} & a_{y} & a_{z} \\
b_{x} & b_{y} & b_{z} 
\end{vmatrix}
$$

$$
|\boldsymbol{a} \times \boldsymbol{b}| = |\boldsymbol{a}| |\boldsymbol{b}| \sin\alpha
$$

# 混合积 (Triple product)

$$
(\boldsymbol{abc}) = (\boldsymbol{a} \times \boldsymbol{b}) \cdot \boldsymbol{c}
$$

$$
(\boldsymbol{abc}) = \begin{vmatrix}
a_{x} & a_{y} & a_{z} \\
b_{x} & b_{y} & b_{z}  \\
c_{x} & c_{y} & c_{z}
\end{vmatrix}
$$
