# 直角坐标

计算一个密度不均匀的三维物体的质量

$$
m = \rho \cdot v = \iiint \limits_{\Omega} \rho(x,y,z) \thinspace \mathrm{d}v
$$

## 先一后二

假设有一个土豆，先把土豆切成竖直的条，再计算整体的体积

$$
\iiint \limits_{\Omega} f(x,y,z) \thinspace \mathrm{d}v =\iint\limits _{D_{xy}}\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y \int _{z_{1}(x,y)}^{z_{2}(x,y)}f(x,y,z) \thinspace \mathrm{d}z
$$

# 先二后一

先把土豆切片

$$
\iiint \limits_{\Omega} f(x,y,z) \thinspace \mathrm{d}v = \int _{z_{1}}^{z_{2}} \thinspace \mathrm{d}z \iint \limits _{D_{z}}f(x,y,z)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y
$$

# 柱坐标

$$
\begin{cases}
x=r\cos \theta, &  0\leq r<+\infty\\
y=r\sin \theta, &  0\leq \theta\leq 2\pi\\
z=z, & -\infty\leq z\leq+\infty
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\mathrm{d}v = r \thinspace \mathrm{d}r\mathrm{d}\theta \mathrm{d}z
$$

$$
\iiint \limits_{\Omega} f(x,y,z) \thinspace \mathrm{d}v = \iiint \limits_{\Omega} f(r\cos \theta,r\sin \theta,z) \thinspace r \mathrm{d}r\mathrm{d}\theta \mathrm{d}z
$$

# 球坐标

$$
\begin{cases}
x=r\sin \varphi\cos \theta, &  0\leq r<+\infty\\
y=r\sin \varphi\sin \theta, &  0\leq \theta\leq 2\pi\\
z=r\cos \varphi, & 0\leq \varphi\leq 2\pi
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\mathrm{d}v = r^{2}\sin \varphi \thinspace \mathrm{d}r\mathrm{d}\varphi\mathrm{d}\theta
$$

$$
\iiint \limits_{\Omega} f(x,y,z) \thinspace \mathrm{d}v = \iiint \limits_{\Omega} f(r\sin \varphi\cos \theta,r\sin \varphi\sin \theta,r\cos \varphi)  r^{2}\sin \varphi \thinspace \mathrm{d}r\mathrm{d}\varphi\mathrm{d}\theta
$$
