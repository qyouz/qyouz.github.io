# 对角线互为倒数：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\sin x & = \frac{1}{\csc x}\\
\cos x & = \frac{1}{\sec x}\\
\cot x & = \frac{1}{\tan x}
\end{aligned}
$$

# 连续三个顶点的关系：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\sin x & = \frac{\cos x}{\cot x}\\
\cos x & = \frac{\cot x}{\csc x}\\
\cot x & = \frac{\csc x}{\sec x}\\
& \dots
\end{aligned}
$$

# 三角形上两个顶点与下一个顶点的关系：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\sin ^{2} x + \cos ^{2} x  = 1 \\
\tan ^{2} x + 1  = \sec ^{2} x\\
1 + \cot ^{2} x = \csc ^{2} x
\end{aligned}
$$

# 记忆口诀

*中下二，下中下，导相乘，积相加*

## 导数

$$
\begin{aligned}
\tan'x & = \sec ^{2} x\\
\sec'x & = \tan x \sec x\\
\cot'x & = -\csc ^{2} x\\
\csc'x & = -\cot x \csc x
\end{aligned}
$$

## 积分

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int \tan x \thinspace \mathrm{d}x & = \ln |\sec x + \sec x|+C\\
\int \sec x \thinspace \mathrm{d}x & = \ln |\sec x + \tan x|+C\\
\int \cot x \thinspace \mathrm{d}x & = -\ln |\csc x + \csc x| +C\\
\int \csc x \thinspace \mathrm{d}x & = -\ln |\csc x + \cot x|+C
\end{aligned}
$$
